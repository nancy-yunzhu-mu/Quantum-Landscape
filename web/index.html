Notes & methodology

Each company is placed in one of four domains — Hardware (qubit systems), Software (algorithms, error correction, post-quantum security), Enabling (control electronics, cryogenics, networking, foundries), and Sensing (quantum metrology). Use the chips above the title to filter by domain.

The funding tier badge buckets total capital raised:

  • Mega — $500M or more raised.
  • Major — $100M–$500M raised.
  • Emerging — under $100M, or undisclosed.

Figures are approximate and compiled from public reporting (company announcements, The Quantum Insider, Crunchbase, SEC filings) as of mid-2026. Private valuations and undisclosed rounds are estimates; public-company figures reflect recent market capitalization, which moves daily. Treat this as a map of the field, not a valuation source.
